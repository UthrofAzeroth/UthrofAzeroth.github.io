About this book

At Mugello, grip is geometry. And geometry does not care about fear.

Elias Thorne drives without ABS. Without traction control. Without forgiveness.

Inside the Optimiser Suite, the Governor protects. The Coach disciplines. The Engineer audits. And the Dreamer builds a lap no human should survive.

When a stitched-best 1:39.842 appears — constructed from fragments of perfection — Elias must decide whether the limit is mechanical… or mental.

In the rain, at Arrabbiata, inside the violence of yaw and brake bias, safety becomes a constraint. And the fastest line may not belong to the living.
