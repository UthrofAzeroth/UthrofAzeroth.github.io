About

A serious speculative-fiction voice with systems, institutions, and consequence at its centre.

Oliver T. Crowe writes intelligent, grounded speculative fiction. His work is drawn to pressure, systems, institutions, and the hidden truths that emerge when people are pushed into difficult decisions.

Across his fiction, he returns to characters working under strain, to official versions that do not fully match reality, and to the practical, material details that make imagined worlds feel real.
